Our Mission

To further the Kingdom of God by equipping leaders through spiritual formation and personal wholeness, and training them to guide those under their care in specific, targeted prayer, completely dependent on God to work the spiritual transactions that free individuals to be everything they were designed to be.

Resolving What Hinders Us

Resolution Ministries is committed to bringing full resolution (spiritual, emotional, relational) to the areas of:

  • Broken relationships
  • Harmful judgments / words of power
  • Consquences of sexual sin
  • Distortions / harmful beliefs
  • Ineffective life patterns
  • Abuse of authority / rebellion
  • Dysfunctional family of origin models
  • Past occult involvement
